342 /** \brief draws a vertical boxplot, with all coordinates/sizes given in coordinates of the given painter,
343 * using the style properties declared in this class. Provide a parameter with \c JKQTP_NAN of you
344 * don't want it to be drawn, or don't know its value
345 *
346 * \param parent parent JKQTBasePlotter of the graph that uses this mix-in (used e.g. for line-width transformation)
347 * \param painter the <a href="http://doc.qt.io/qt-5/qpainter.html">QPainter</a> to draw to
348 * \param xp x-coordinate of the boxplot center
349 * \param xpleft x-coordinate of the boxplot box left edge
350 * \param xpright x-coordinate of the boxplot box right edge
351 * \param minp y-coordinate of the minimum (lower whisker)
352 * \param q25p y-coordinate of the 25% quartile (lower box border)
353 * \param medianp y-coordinate the median
354 * \param q75p y-coordinate of the 75% quartile (upper box border)
355 * \param maxp y-coordinate of the maximum (upper whisker)
356 * \param meanp y-coordinate of the mean (symbol or line)
357 * \param notchLowerp y-coordinate of the lower (near \a qe5p ) end of the notch interval
358 * \param notchUpperp y-coordinate of the upper (near \a q75p ) end of the notch interval
359 */
